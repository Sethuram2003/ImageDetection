How It Works

  1. Line Detection: Detects line segments using Canny edge detection and Probabilistic Hough Transform
  2. Intersection Analysis: Computes intersections of all line pairs to find convergence points
  3. Clustering: Groups intersection points using DBSCAN to identify true vanishing points
  4. Classification: Analyzes VP count and distribution to assess geometric consistency

Understanding Results

  • 1 VP: Strong single perspective - typical of real photographs
  • 2 VPs: Two-point perspective - common in architectural photos
  • 3+ VPs: Complex geometry - may indicate AI-generated content or complex scenes
  • 0 VPs: Minimal parallel structure - organic/natural scenes
